Later, I moved into the Automation Framework Development team, where I played a key role in developing new tools from concept to release. I also got the opportunity to conduct department-level training sessions and participate in the interview process, primarily focusing on Python and automation.


Being eager to learn, I have also completed an MBA in Project Management, which I believe will be valuable for handling multiple projects in the future. As I progressed into senior roles, I trained myself in advanced Python concepts and certifications, which I now use to develop automation and data analysis tools that enhance efficiency in my work.
